Math Practice Apps

Interactive math exercises and learning tools

Basic Arithmetic Practice

The app focuses on practicing counting forwards and backwards using a number line up to 1000, with special emphasis on transitions over decades and hundreds. It includes interactive activities for counting in 2s, 3s, 5s, and 10s starting from any number—not just zero—as well as exercises to build fluency with number bonds.

Fill in the Gaps

Practice counting with helpful hints! Some numbers are already filled in, just complete the missing ones. Great for getting started and building confidence.

Finish the Pattern

Ready for a challenge? Start with the first number and complete the whole sequence on your own. Perfect for kids who are growing confident with their counting skills.
